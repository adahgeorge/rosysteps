OUR MISSION STATEMENTS:

Rosysteps International Schools are dedicated to our students cognitive, psychomotor and affective development in an enabling environment that fosters self confidence, leadership skills and caring disposition. We aim at developing, producing a total child. We intend to achieve this through a sound-based educational technique, modeling, mentoring, motivating and monitoring. This helps our pupils/ students to reach their highest potentials and grow in respect for themselves, others and our earth. We provide our children with solid foundation in all academic areas, and this facilities their knowledge, and help them to integrate this knowledge in their everyday life.



OUR VISION:

Rosysteps International Schools vision is to be the best school known for producing change-agents that will impact our society Secondly, to be the school preferred by all and sundry and the society at large.
