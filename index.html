Extra Curricular Activities

At Rosysteps International Schools, we incorporate exra-curricular activities that enable our students and pupils to stay up-to-date with latest technologies and global standards.

Learn More
JETS Club

JETS club is an acronym for Junior Engineers Technicians and Scientists club. It is a club designed to promote interests in Science, Engineering and Technology by engaging it's members in science fairs, quizzes, lectures, excursions, debates etc.

Programmers' Club

Programmers Club is a group of students founded solely for the purpose of computer programming and coding skills. We develop Web / mobile Apps, Games and program robotics using Python programming language.
